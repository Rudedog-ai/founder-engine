## Services Offered

### Car Servicing
- Individual and specific service requirements based on make, model, age, mileage
- Will discuss any additional work before proceeding

  - Alternate with Small Service every ~10,000 miles
  - Comprehensive service includes: engine oil change, oil filter, air filter, pollen filter, fuel filter (where applicable)
- Uses Mobil Super 3000 oils (5W-40 and 5W-30 variants)
- "Please get in touch for a detailed quotation" — no fixed prices on website
- Free health check available

### MOT Testing
- MOT for cars (Class IV — up to 3.5 tonnes), motorbikes, scooters (Class I & II), light commercials
- All UK cars need first MOT at 3 years, then annually
- Average MOT takes about 45 minutes
- Customers can watch the MOT on screen in the waiting room
- Service advisors can check if your car has a valid MOT
- Can book MOT online
- Can combine MOT with service in same booking

### Tyres
- Tyre supply and fitting (details on services page)

### Wheel Alignment
- Also called "tracking"
- Alignment to manufacturer specifications
- Benefits: better fuel economy, smoother handling, better tyre life
- Free wheel balance included with services
- Symptoms of misalignment: pulling to one side, uneven tyre wear, steering/suspension issues

### Repairs & Breakdowns
- Diagnostic capability using up-to-date Bosch diagnostic equipment
- Warning light diagnosis
- Brake pad and disc replacement
- "We can diagnose and repair your car with the least hassle and disruption"
- Will quote before proceeding with work

### Motorbikes

### Scooters
