## 🎯 Core Mission

**Transform how non-tech founders run their businesses by making AI-native operations accessible, trustworthy, and repeatable.**

You'll own the product experience for a £20-25K transformation engagement that takes scattered, manual businesses and turns them into data-driven, AI-assisted operations—without breaking anything along the way.

This is NOT a typical SaaS PM role. You're building a **productized transformation service**—more like designing a surgery protocol than shipping features. Every engagement must be:
- **Repeatable** (not bespoke consulting)
- **Measurable** (clear ROI, not vague "insights")

## 🧠 Required Skills (Non-Negotiable)

### 1. Founder Empathy (Essential)
You've either:
- Been a founder (ideally non-tech, 10-200 employees)
- Worked closely with SMB founders (consultancy, agency, fractional exec)
- Built products FOR non-tech users (not developers, not enterprises)

**Why:** You need to feel their chaos. Scattered Google Drive. Manual spreadsheets. No process. No time. High skepticism. This isn't enterprise software—it's intimate, high-trust transformation.

**Test question:** "What's the #1 reason founders stay manual instead of adopting tools?"
- ❌ Wrong answer: "They don't know better tools exist"
- ✅ Right answer: "Switching costs are hidden (learning curve, migration risk, team resistance) and ROI is unclear"

### 2. AI Product Sense (Critical)
You know:
- What AI can do (RAG, structured extraction, agentic workflows)
- What AI can't do (perfect accuracy, complex reasoning, replacing humans entirely)
- How to design for AI limitations (confidence scores, source citations, human-in-loop)
- Cost economics (Haiku vs Sonnet vs Opus, when to use which)

**Why:** Founder Engine uses Claude for fact extraction, ElevenLabs for voice, pgvector for RAG. You need to know what's hype vs what's real.

**Test question:** "Our two-pass ingestion costs $10 for 10K files. How would you make it cheaper without sacrificing quality?"
- ❌ Wrong answer: "Use GPT-3.5 instead of Claude"
- ✅ Right answer: "Add metadata filters (file size, type) before Haiku call to reduce LLM calls by 30-40%"

### 3. Outcome-Driven Design (Essential)
You think in **outcomes** (time saved, revenue unlocked, cost avoided), not **outputs** (features shipped, metrics improved).

**Why:** Founders pay £20K for transformation, not software. They need to see ROI, not feature lists.

**Test question:** "A founder says 'I don't have time to verify 400 extracted facts.' How do you respond?"
- ❌ Wrong answer: "We'll add bulk verification (mark all as correct)"
- ✅ Right answer: "We'll highlight the 20 HIGH-IMPACT facts (revenue, churn, runway) and auto-verify the rest with LOW confidence scores"

### 4. B2B Sales Understanding (Important)
You've sold or helped sell high-touch B2B ($10K+ deals) and know:
- How to articulate ROI before purchase
- How to build trust with skeptical buyers
- How to handle "I need to think about it" (not "no", but not "yes")
- How to create urgency without being pushy

**Why:** This isn't product-led growth. It's relationship-driven, founder-to-founder selling.

**Test question:** "A founder says 'Looks cool but I'm slammed right now.' What do you do?"
- ❌ Wrong answer: "Follow up in 3 months"
- ✅ Right answer: "Sounds like you need this MOST. What if we onboard you in 3 hours spread over 3 weeks? 1 hour/week, we'll do the rest."

### 5. Scrappy Execution (Essential)
You've shipped products with:
- No designers (use Figma + ChatGPT)
- No PMs (you're the first PM)
- No budget (self-funded startup, every pound counts)
- No time (founder is time-poor, you need to move fast)

**Why:** This is a self-funded startup. Ruari's funding it via TP ICAP salary for 12-18 months. Every pound and hour matters.

**Test question:** "We need a founder onboarding video. Budget: £0. Time: 3 days. Go."
- ❌ Wrong answer: "Hire a video production agency (£5K)"
- ✅ Right answer: "I'll record a Loom walkthrough, use Descript to clean it up, add captions, and ship it. If it works, we'll polish it later."
